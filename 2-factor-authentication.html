What is AccessWerx?


Two-Factor Authentication is a strong security practice that involves two types of validation in order to access confidential or sensitive information or systems.


Passwords are only as secure as the people who know them. Leaked, lost, or stolen data can reveal sensitive passwords and user accounts that can jeopardize security and confidentiality, and cause major issues and public mistrust.

AccessWerx is the integration of a two-factor authentication system, which is defined as:

  1. Something you know, such as a password.
  2. Something you have, such as a card, token, or mobile phone.
