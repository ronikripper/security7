Dedicated Internet Access

Dedicated Ethernet Built For Secure Bandwidth And Savings.

You need a reliable, scalable business data network, not a huge drain on your IT budget. Comcast Business offers fast, private and easy-to-configure dedicated Ethernet connectivity in several cost-effective configurations, depending on your needs.

Why Comcast Dedicated Internet Access?

In business today, organizations utilize the Internet as an essential business tool. Ethernet-based Dedicated Internet Access (DIA) services provide many benefits over T1-based DIA services, including higher bandwidth, and cost-effective Internet access to help them to manage their IT costs while they grow their business.

How Does Ethernet Based Dedicated Internet Work?

Designed for organizations needing more bandwidth and a highly secure, dedicated connection between their existing Local Area Network (LAN) and the public Internet, Ethernet Dedicated Internet provides an Ethernet Virtual Connection (EVC) from the customer’s location to a Comcast Internet Point of Presence (POP) router. Our Ethernet interface enables true plug-and-play compatibility with your LAN.
