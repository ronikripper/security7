Many employees outside the technology department are unfamiliar with the best practices for keeping their information secure. This can lead to costly data breaches, phishing attacks, downloading of malicious content, and various other hacks. Therefore a need exists to train employees about how to keep their own and company information secure.

Security7 Networks provides easily accessible security awareness and technical training for our clients and employees. Our Information Security education offers foundational training to build core skills, and additional resources to provide expanded knowledge on various cyber security topics.

Topics include essential information security issues such as protecting cardholder data, malware, social engineering, password security, email security, physical security, mobile device security, PCI compliance, phishing, and security while traveling.

A Simple and Universal Approach to Security Education

Security7 Networks' information security training is intended for all employee branches including customer service, human resources, legal, marketing, finance, operations, and salespeople who have access to an organization's computer systems and information.

Security7’s training and awareness provides simple yet enlightening delivery of fundamental security information and practices. Real-world examples and exercises engage the learner with applicable content.

PCI Essentials – The Definitive Awareness Training

Developed in conjunction with the PCI Security Standards Council, the PCI Essentials online learning program helps reduce the impact of threats to payment card security by equipping staff with the knowledge needed to keep customer and company data secure.

PCI Essentials comprises 10 highly interactive modules, approximately 15 minutes long, each focusing on a specific area of cardholder and information security. The training is designed to address all of the security awareness topics needed for compliance with the training requirements of PCI DSS.
